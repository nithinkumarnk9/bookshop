Continuous Discovery Habits: It refers to the practices that product teams, especially product managers, designers, and researchers, adopt to consistently learn about user needs, market changes, and validate ideas throughout the product lifecycle. Instead of limiting research to the early phases of product development, continuous discovery integrates learning as a daily or weekly routine to guide decisions and improve products incrementally. This approach ensures that products evolve in alignment with real customer needs and evolving market conditions.
